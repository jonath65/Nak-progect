School History

Nakivale Secondary School was founded by UNHCR, ONE forward-thinking visionary who shared a deep belief in the transformative power of education. With a shared commitment to providing quality learning, they laid the foundation for what would become a thriving institution. Built from the ground up with unwavering dedication and passion, the school has steadily grown and evolved over the years. Today, it stands as one of the finest learning establishments, continuously shaping and inspiring generations of students to achieve their fullest potential and make meaningful contributions to society.

Vision && Mission

And just like any school, Nakivale Secondary School has a a vision & mission that reflects what it does and how it does it, feel free to explore them down here.

Vision:
To be a leading institution that nurtures academic and moral excellence.

Meaning:
It means our institution aims to be a top organization that promotes both academic achievement and strong ethical values. We strive to provide students with the knowledge and skills to succeed academically. At the same time, we focus on fostering integrity, responsibility, and good character. Our goal is to shape well-rounded individuals who excel in both their studies and personal conduct.

Mission:
To provide quality education, foster innovation, and develop leaders of tomorrow.

Meaning:
Our goal is to deliver high-quality education that prepares students for success. We encourage creativity and new ideas to solve problems and drive progress. We focus on developing leadership skills that will empower students in their future careers. Ultimately, we aim to shape individuals who will lead and make a positive impact in the world.

Students Life

Through a balanced approach to education, we aim to empower our students with the knowledge, confidence, and resilience needed to thrive in an ever-evolving world.
